3
Click Connect — admin opens instantly

What happens when you push:

        

          
1 Admin panel reads your latest index.html from GitHub

          
2 Your new/edited articles are injected into the articles array

          
3 SEO meta, FAQs and structured data are merged in

          
4 The updated file is committed to your repo

          
5 GitHub Pages rebuilds — live in ~30 seconds
